ABOUT US

Sidcup Family Golf is a family-owned and operated golf facility in Sidcup, South East London. We provide a variety of facilities, including top-tier golf courses, a comfortable golf course, and a beautiful yard. Our team of experienced golfers and amateur golfers work tirelessly to keep our facility at the top of the golfing charts.

TopRacer Range

The Toptracer Range is a popular golf course in Sidcup, South East London. Located just 5 minutes from the golf facility, it offers excellent views of the city and the surrounding area.

ADVENTURE GOLF

Our adventure golf course is designed for those looking to explore new golf areas and discover new opportunities. It offers a variety of parks, trails, and amenities, making it a great choice for those seeking a new adventure.

GOLF LESSONS

Our golf lessons are designed to help you develop your skills and improve your game. Our friendly and knowledgeable instructors will guide you through the golf course, helping you become a top-tier golfer.
